How Mental Health Can Save Businesses

A negative working environment may lead to multiple problems, workplaces that promote mental health and support people are more likely to reduce absenteeism, increase productivity and benefit from associated economic gains.

  • US$ 1 trillion
    lost each year

    Globally, an estimated 264 million people suffer from depression, recent studies estimates that mental health disorders cost the global economy US$ 1 trillion each year in lost productivity

  • 120,000 deaths
    each year on US only

    Stressful jobs contribute to 120,000 deaths each year and cost U.S. businesses up to $190 billion in health care costs.

  • US$ 4 return
    for each $1 spent

    For every US$ 1 put into scaled up treatment for common mental disorders, there is a return of US$ 4 in improved health and productivity.
